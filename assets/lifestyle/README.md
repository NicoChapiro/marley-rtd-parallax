# Future lifestyle image slots

This folder is reserved for future Marley Coffee Ready to Drink campaign photography. Do not add placeholder stock photography or unapproved image assets.

Recommended filenames when legally approved production assets are ready:

- `outdoor-desktop.webp` / `outdoor-mobile.webp` — mountain or outdoor lifestyle moment.
- `onthego-desktop.webp` / `onthego-mobile.webp` — person holding a can or daily movement moment.
- `closeup-desktop.webp` / `closeup-mobile.webp` — cold can, condensation, or hand detail.

Suggested crops:

- Desktop: 16:9 or 3:2.
- Mobile: 9:16 or 4:5.

Optimization guidance:

- Keep each optimized image under 350 KB when possible.
- Export as WebP after color and legal approval.
- Confirm all talent, location, product, and usage rights before production deployment.
